Carrel — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026 · Last updated: June 12, 2026

Carrel ("the extension," "we") is a browser extension that saves web pages to your Notion workspace. This policy explains exactly what data Carrel handles. It is operated by Toolwright, contact hello@toolwright.studio.

In short: Carrel is local-first. We do not run any servers and we do not collect, transmit, or have access to your data. The only place your data is ever sent is the Notion API, to the workspace you connect.

What Carrel stores on your device

The following are stored only in your browser's local extension storage (chrome.storage.local) and never leave your device except as described below:

  • Your Notion integration token (used to authorize saving to Notion).
  • Your preferences and default database selection.
  • A cached list of your Notion databases and their property names, to populate the clip form.
  • Any clips queued while you are offline, until they sync.

What Carrel sends, and to whom

When you click Clip, Carrel reads the content of the current page and sends it, together with the title, tags, and properties you choose, to the Notion API (api.notion.com) using your integration token, in order to create a page in the database you selected. That is the only network destination. Carrel reads page content only on the active tab and only when you initiate a clip.

What Carrel does not do

  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or fingerprinting.
  • No data sent to us or any third party other than Notion.
  • No collection of browsing history or activity across sites.
  • No selling or sharing of any data. We have no access to it.

Third parties

Carrel interacts only with Notion, the service you explicitly connect. Your use of Notion is governed by Notion's own privacy policy.

Data retention and deletion

Because data is stored locally, you control it. Remove your token in Settings, clear the extension's storage, or uninstall the extension to delete everything Carrel stores. Pages already saved to Notion are managed by you in Notion.

Security

Your token is stored in your browser's local extension storage and is transmitted only to Notion over HTTPS. Keep your device and browser profile secure, as local storage is accessible to your browser profile.

Children

Carrel is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

Chrome Web Store Limited Use

Carrel's use of information obtained through the extension adheres to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. Data accessed by Carrel is used only to provide the extension's single, user-facing purpose — saving pages to your Notion — and for no other purpose.
