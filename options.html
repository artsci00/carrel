Carrel

Reliable, local-first research capture.

1 · Connect Notion

  1. Open notion.so/my-integrations and create a new internal integration. Copy its secret (starts with ntn_ or secret_).
  2. In Notion, open the database you want to clip into → ••• menu → Connections → add your integration. (Do this for every database you want as a destination.)
  3. Paste the secret below and test the connection.

Stored only in this browser's local storage. Carrel sends it to api.notion.com and nowhere else.
